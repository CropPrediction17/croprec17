Muskmelon

Muskmelon is grown in China,Turkey,India and in many other countries.

In India muskmelon growing state includes Punjab, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Andhra Pradesh. Muskmelons grow best on well-drained, sandy loam soils, with a pH between 6.0 and 6.5. Muskmelons can grow better at the temperatures range in between 180C to 25C and temperatures below 12C the plant growth may be halted with poor performance and muskmelons can withstand upto 45C temperature among some varieties. Apply FYM 20 t/ha, NPK 40:60:30 kg/ha as basal and N @ 40 kg/ha 30 days after sowing. Apply Azospirillum and Phosphobacteria @ 2 kg/ha and Pseudomonas @ 2.5 kg/ha along with FYM 50 kg and neem cake 100 kg before last ploughing.
